🎮 How to Play

  • Turn Loop: Press 🎲 ROLL to find your move points, then press a directional arrow to initiate your run.
  • Full Sprint: You launch forward the entire dice length in that one straight path.
  • Frogs Ecosystem: Exactly 5 frogs (🐸) live on the board. Overlapping them consumes them and extends your tail length.
  • Obstacles: Blocked paths or self-collisions highlight grid boundary restrictions and warnings in red.
  • Winning Rule: Reach square 100 with **at least 10 frogs** in your tally to win! Reaching it early resets you back out onto the field.
